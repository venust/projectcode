What is a Multipotentialite?

Multipotentialite is the state of having many exceptional talents.Anyone or more of which could make for a great career for that person. Gifted children are often but not always fall under multipotentialite.Their advanced interllectual nature and intense curosity makes them prime candidates for excelling in multiple areas. This can be a blessing and a curse. On the bright side, they have many realistic options for future careers.But on the down side some of them will struggle mightily trying to decide which choice to make. The bottom line and simply put Multipotentialites are people who have many interest.
